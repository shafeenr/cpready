CPReady

The Design

Simple parts for a complex problem

Our design consists of

  • An Arduino Uno -- Microcontroller that acts as the hub and master of other components
  • A Breadboard -- Distributes power to our parts
  • A Piezo Buzzer -- Buzzes the rhythm of which users perform CPR to
  • A 16x2 Display -- Notifies the user of the pressure of their compressions
  • A Force Sensitive Resistor -- Detects the pressure of the user's compressions
