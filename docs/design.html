CPReady

The Design

Simple parts for a complex problem

Our design consists of

  • An Arduino Uno -- Microcontroller that acts as the hub and master of other components
  • A Breadboard -- Distributes power to our parts
  • A Piezo Buzzer -- Buzzes the rhythm of which users perform CPR to
  • A 16x2 Display -- Notifies the user of the pressure of their compressions
  • A Force Sensitive Resistor -- Detects the pressure of the user's compressions
  • The Custom CPReady Case and Holder -- Holds all our components together

While we went through our prototypes for many different ideas, we went through problems like the placement of the LCD and breadboard, how we'd keep our parts compact and much more.

To combat this, we created a hinge design to protect our Arduino and breadboard while keeping the LCD display on top. This design allows us to easily adjust parts.

However, with our hinge design, we had fears that the top component would collapse onto the protective case for the Arduino, so we added stands on the front to keep the display and breadboard up.

In conclusion, our parts now fit snug and compact, fit for any first aid kit.
